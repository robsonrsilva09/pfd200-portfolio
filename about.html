From São Paulo to a UK FdSc

I am originally from São Paulo, Brazil, and my journey into computing has grown from curiosity into a serious career direction. Living and studying in the UK has helped me develop discipline, independence, and a stronger understanding of how technology can solve real problems.

Through my FdSc Computing course, I am building foundations in web development, databases, programming, cybersecurity, and software design. I enjoy turning ideas into working interfaces and learning how each technical decision affects usability, performance, and security.

My motivation is to become a developer who creates useful digital products with intention. I want my work to combine clear visual design, reliable code, and practical value for users, clients, and future teams I collaborate with.
